The Challenge Our Approach The Work The Outcome

The Challenge

Sexual health remains a sensitive topic for many audiences, making it important for Ebony to communicate its message in a way that felt approachable, relevant, and engaging while strengthening brand awareness.

Our Approach

We developed a campaign that positioned Ebony as a brand that encourages responsible choices through clear, relatable, and culturally relevant communication. The focus was on creating messaging that connected with audiences while making conversations around protection more accessible.

The Work

The campaign was brought to life through creative assets designed to capture attention, communicate the importance of protection, and reinforce Ebony’s brand presence across key audience touchpoints.

The Outcome

The campaign strengthened Ebony’s visibility and helped reinforce the brand’s position within the sexual wellness category through a message centered around awareness, responsibility, and informed choices.
