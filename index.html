About Insel

Crafting award-winning campaigns since 1998

Insel has grown into one of Ghana's most recognised creative agencies, built on one belief: to be good, really good, requires focus.

We take consumer insight and turn it into communication that brings results. That means sitting with a brand's real problem before we touch a headline, then working with a trusted network of production and media partners to get the idea out into the world at scale.

From FMCG launches to city-wide brand campaigns, our name has sat behind some of the work Ghanaians actually remember.
