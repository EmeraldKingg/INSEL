FMCG · Brand Campaign

Convincing Ghana to drop that "yam."

Tigo wanted more people to embrace smartphones. Rather than talking specs, Insel created a culturally relevant campaign that turned upgrading into something everyone was talking about.

Client
Tigo Ghana
Sector
FMCG · Brand Campaign
Services
Brand Strategy, Creative, Media Planning, TV, Digital, Outdoor
Format
TV · Print · Outdoor
The Challenge Our Approach The Work The Outcome

The Challenge

Many consumers still relied on feature phones despite growing smartphone availability. Tigo needed a campaign that would change perceptions and make upgrading feel desirable.

Our Approach

Rather than focusing on technical specifications, the creative idea tapped into everyday humour and social pressure. The campaign used relatable storytelling to make smartphone ownership feel modern and aspirational.

The Work

The campaign rolled out across television, digital and other media, featuring memorable characters and dialogue that resonated with audiences nationwide.

The Outcome

    One of Ghana's best-known advertising campaigns.
    Popularised the phrase "Drop That Yam."
    Helped reinforce the cultural nickname "yam" for feature phones.
