Energy · Brand Campaign

Making an energy brand feel human, not industrial.

Brand storytelling and outdoor creative that gave VPower a personality beyond the product spec sheet.

Client
VPower
Sector
Energy · Brand Campaign
Services
Brand Storytelling, Creative, Outdoor
Format
Outdoor · TV · Social
The Challenge Our Approach The Work The Outcome

The Challenge

Energy-sector brands tend to talk in specs and reliability claims that all sound the same to the average consumer.

Our Approach

We shifted the conversation from what the product does to who it's for, building a campaign narrative around the people who depend on it daily.

The Work

The campaign ran across outdoor sites in high-traffic areas, supported by a TV spot and social content extending the same story.

The Outcome

VPower's advertising moved from generic category messaging to a recognisable, story-led brand presence.
