Introducing a new telecom brand to a competitive market.

Launch creative and a go-to-market media plan built to get Airtel Tigo Medger noticed on day one.

Services

The Challenge Our Approach The Work The Outcome

The Challenge

Bringing two major telecom brands together meant managing customer expectations while creating a clear identity for the newly merged business. The challenge was to communicate change in a way that felt seamless, positive, and customer-focused.

Our Approach

We focused on the idea of connection showing that the merger was not just about combining networks, but about bringing people, communities, and opportunities closer together. Every communication touchpoint was designed to build trust and excitement around the new chapter.

The Work

The campaign rolled out across multiple channels, combining mass media, digital communication, and customer engagement initiatives to announce the merger, explain the benefits, and reinforce the strength of the combined network.

The Outcome

The campaign successfully introduced the merger to customers with a clear and unified message, helping position the new brand as a stronger, more connected telecommunications provider.
